Masato Tamura

masato.tamura [at] hal.hitachi.com

I am a senior researcher at Hitachi America, Ltd. Research & Development Group, working on developing computer vision technologies. I am also a graduate student of University of Tsukuba and am pursuing a PhD in Computer Science under the supervision of Prof. Kazuhiro Fukui.

I previously studied digital circuit design at the Ikeda Lab of the University of Tokyo, where I received BE and ME degrees under the supervision of Prof. Makoto Ikeda.
